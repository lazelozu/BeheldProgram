Cast



Vidisha Agarwalla '22
Cecilia

Vidisha Agarwalla is a fifth-year doing a Plus One Masters in Creative Practice Leadership. Previous Northeastern credits include Cece in This Is Treatment, Bacchant in The Bacchae, Lighting Designer in Water by the Spoonful and Producer in What Rough Beast for the New Renaissance Theatre Company. Vidisha is also President of the NRTC. Originally from Singapore, she strives to diversify the theatre and performing arts communities. She loves New Ren, the people, and the mission with all her heart.




Katie Copeland
Camilla, Teresa, [Cousin, Mistress]

Sarah Waggoner
Marina, [Girl, New Servant]

Previous theatre credits include Northeastern Theatre Department’s production of “This Is Treatment” (Ms. Anita) and costume build crew.




Ella Dunn
Sole Mother, [Sister]

Crew



Jonathan Baron '24
Director

Jonathan is a third-year student majoring in Political Science and Economics. He is from Los Angeles, but prefers the Boston snow to sunny SoCal beaches (for some reason). In his free time, he enjoys reading, creative writing, and calligraphy. On stage, he has performed stand-up comedy and improv theatre, but this is his first time directing a play — and he’s very excited about it!




Chi Nguyen
Assistant Director

Chi is a second-year exchange student currently majoring in Communications. She has had experience directing plays in Vietnam, her home country, but this is her first time joining a theatre production at Northeastern. She hopes to get more involved in the theatre scene in Boston and is excited to develop her skills as a theatremaker.




Ashley DiLorenzo '25
Event Photographer

Ashley is a second year Media and Screen Studies and Theatre major with a songwriting minor from Queens, NY. This is her first time as New Ren’s official photographer after taking the production photos for and costume designing Gruesome Playground Injuries. She’s also on the Silver Masque E-Board (Tech Squad) and her ten-minute play “The Tale of […] Nine Lives” will be produced this December (woo!). Previous Northeastern productions include This is Treatment (Ms. Caroline), Love and Information (Actor 5), and currently Problem Play (Props Director) and NUStage’s Great Comet (Dolokhov).




Lou Conrad '23
Set Designer, Captions Operator

Lou is a 5th year combined major in Theatre and Media Screen Studies. Their previous Northeastern credits include Three Sisters (Ferapont), Urinetown (Mr McQueen) and working backstage in the scene shop and costume department. They also performed in their first professional show at the Apollinaire Theater in Chelsea, Hir, as Max. During the pandemic Lou participated in a few performances over zoom, but sort of disappeared from theater for a bit. They are overjoyed and grateful to be back and working with this group of amazing individuals in person. Theater is beautiful and important. Please keep others safe during this time so we can continue to share why.




Dani Balkin
Props Director, Wardrobe Crew, Deck Crew

Dani Balkin is in her second production role at Northeastern. She’s an American Sign Language English-Interpreting Major with a theater minor. She’s had a great time with New Ren and would like to thank the Beheld team for their time and energy. Enjoy the show!




Ivy Kee
Playwright

Ivy Kee (Playwright) is a fifth year Theatre Performance major with minors in Psychology and Performance & Social Change. She has been a member of New Ren since 2019, previously serving as the Secretary/Vice President from 2019-2020 and acting in productions such as Joker’s Wild (Hanzo) and Stop Kiss (Sara). This is the first play she has ever written; and she will be seen next in the NU Theatre Department’s production of Problem Play (Courtney).
